Storm Crow Tavern


Approx. 2 hours

Kid-friendly

Indoors

$



Although tehcnically a bar, the Stormcrow offers service to all ages prior to 8pm which is why this activity makes it on both our kid-friendly and 19+ itineraries! If you love movies, comics, games, and geek culture, this is the place to be. Grab your friends for a pint and a round of Cards Against Humanity, or roll a 20-sided die for a myster shot! Be sure to check out their website for fun weekly events like pub trivia or karaoke.
