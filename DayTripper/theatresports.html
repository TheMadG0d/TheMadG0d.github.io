Theatre Sports


Approx. 2 hours

Kid-friendly

Indoors

$9-$25



Located on Granville Island, the Vancouver Theatresports league is world famous for its comedy improv shows. If you are a fan of Who's Line is it Anyway? be sure to check out a show, where Colin Mochrie and Ryan Stiles got their start! For a picturesque start to the evening, catch an aquabus from downtown to this quaint side of town.
