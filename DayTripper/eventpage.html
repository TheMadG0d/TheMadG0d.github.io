Bike the Seawall


Minimum rental time 1 hour

Kid-friendly

Outdoors

$18.50+ per bike



Experience Vancouver's beautiful coastline with this scenic tour around Stanley Park. Begin your journey at the Reckless Bike Store near the Yaletown Roundhouse to rent your bike and helmet, then head right to the trail! Pay attention to the signage to make sure you stay on the bike path and off the pedestrian paths!
