Science World


Approx. 4 hours

Kid-friendly

Outdoors

$15-$23 per person



The Telus World of Science (or "Science World" as it's better known to locals) is a fantastic museum of science located in picturesque Olympic village, just steps from the Main Street skytrain. There's plenty to see for both young and old including an omnimax theatre and rotating exhibition that changes frequently!
