Aquarium


Approx. 4 hours

Kid-friendly

Indoors

$22-$39 per person



Since opening in 1956, the Vancouver Aquarium has connected more than 40 million people from around the world to our oceans. As a non-profit, they also lead a huge conservation effort. Head down early to avoid the crowds and spend an afternoon viewing everything from jellyfish to penguins in scenic Stanley Park!
