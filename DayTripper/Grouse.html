Grouse Mountain


Approx. 3 hours

Kid-friendly

Outdoors

$16+ per person



Just 15 minutes from downtown, Vancouver's premier attraction offers the perfect destination for a truly rewarding and memorable experience. Popular in both summer and winter, the mountain provides a variety of cultural, educational and outdoor adventures to choose from including a live black bear enclosure! Park and catch the gondola at the base, or if you are in the mood for a hike try your endurance on the infamous Grous Grind trail to the top.
