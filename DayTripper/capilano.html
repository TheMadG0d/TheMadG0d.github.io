Capilano Suspension Bridge


Approx. 2 hours

Kid-friendly

Outdoors

$15+ per person



The Capilano Suspension Bridge crosses the Capilano River in the District of North Vancouver. The current bridge is 140 metres long and 70 metres above the river. A wonderful way to catch some scenic views, fresh air, and the perfect shot for instagram!
